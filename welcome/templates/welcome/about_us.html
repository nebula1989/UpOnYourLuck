About the Project

UpOnYourLuck is a website created as a final capstone project by a team of computer science students at Wake Technical Community College in Raleigh, North Carolina.

The goal of UpOnYourLuck is to provide QR Code stickers to people who perform or ask for money on the street. When a passerby scans a user's QR code, they will be redirected to a profile page where they can read more about the person, and even donate.

Despite being a school project, UpOnYourLuck is a serious undertaking for us as we all strived to create something practical and helpful.

How You Can Help

Please reach out to us using our contact form here if you would like to help!

For our users who find themselves homeless, they may not have a physical address and would need a place to pick up their QR Code.

If you're associated with a shelter, food bank, or non-profit, please consider allowing users who are homeless to pick up their QR Code Stickers at your location.
