The Idea

"How can you save the world you have not seen if you can't save the community you have seen?.‚Äù - Pete Seeger

UpOnYourLuck was created as a final capstone project by a team of computer science students at Wake Technical Community College in Raleigh, North Carolina.

UpOnYourLuck assists those who are facing homelessness by providing a platform to share their story and receive donations from those willing to give.

Despite being a school project, UpOnYourLuck is a serious undertaking for us.

There is full intention to bring this project to fruition, we feel it would be a vital resource to people who so often desperately need vital resources.
