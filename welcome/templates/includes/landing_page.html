Make a Profile See Users in the Area

    Who can benefit from this app?

  • People who have fallen on hard times.
  • Musicians and other artists who perform in public areas.
  • Street vendors.

How It Works

  1. Create a Profile and add a photo, biography, and CashApp or Venmo link to your profile page.
  2. Request a QR Coded Sticker to be mailed, or print one yourself.
  3. Place the QR Code Sticker on any sign while you are busking, selling art, or asking for general donations.
  4. When a Passerby scans your QR Code sticker, they will be taken to your profile page where they can read about you, and even donate!
  5. We do not take any percentage of the donation, we simply link your personal payment app to people who check out your page.
