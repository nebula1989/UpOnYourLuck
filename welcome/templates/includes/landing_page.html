    How can this app help you?

  • Gives passersby a chance to give you a donation when they have no cash.
  • Gives you a chance to tell the world who you are, and how you got in your current situation.

How It Works

  1. Create a Profile and add a photo, biography, and CashApp or Venmo link to your profile page.
  2. Request your personalized QR Coded Sticker or print one yourself.
  3. Place the QR Code Sticker on any sign while you are busking, or asking for donations.
  4. When a Passerby scans your code, they will be taken to your profile page.
  5. Need Help? Click Here.
